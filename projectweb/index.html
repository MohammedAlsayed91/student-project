About helen



HELEN project is a comprehensive establishment located in the vibrant city of Irbid, targeting graduate students from high school as well as all girls aspiring to study at any of the accredited universities in Irbid. We believe in the power of education, personal growth, and community. Our project is dedicated to creating a transformative living and learning experience for young women in the vibrant city of Irbid. With a range of exceptional facilities and services, we aim to empower our residents to reach their full potential, foster a sense of belonging, and cultivate a passion for lifelong learning.


OUR MISSION

IS TO PROVIDE A SAFE AND COMFORTABLE ENVIRONMENT FOR GIRLS TO LIVE, STUDY, AND RELAX.

Our mission at HELEN project is to provide a dynamic and enriching environment that supports the educational journey and holistic development of young women. We are committed to offering exceptional facilities, innovative programs, and personalized support to ensure that every resident thrives academically, socially, and emotionally. Through our unwavering dedication to excellence, we aim to empower individuals to become confident, compassionate, and globally minded leaders who make a positive impact on society.


OUR VISION

IS TO CREATE A NURTURING AND EMPOWERING SPACE THAT FOSTERS PERSONAL GROWTH, ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE, AND HOLISTIC WELL-BEING.

Our vision at Helen project is to be a trailblazer in creating a transformative living and learning experience for young women. We strive to inspire and empower individuals to reach their full potential, fostering a community that celebrates diversity, nurtures personal growth, and cultivates a passion for lifelong learning. Our vision is to create a vibrant and inclusive environment where dreams are realized and future leaders are shaped.
